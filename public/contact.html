Frequently Asked Questions

How can I inquire about booking or partnering with Jenks Productions?

We love hearing from new collaborators and event hosts. Simply reach out through our contact form or give us a call. Our team typically responds within one business day to discuss your vision and how we can bring it to life.

Who are your events designed for?

Our live entertainment events are crafted for everyone—families, community members, and corporate groups alike. Whether you're looking for a weekend outing or planning a corporate gathering, our shows create memorable experiences for all ages.

What types of experiences do you produce?

We specialize in a vibrant mix of live entertainment events including artisan markets, craft fairs, food festivals, and seasonal celebrations. Each year we host 16 unique shows across Connecticut, each with its own distinct personality and community focus.

How far in advance should I reach out for planning discussions?

We recommend reaching out at least 2-3 months in advance for corporate or large-scale event planning. For vendor applications and community partnerships, we open registration seasonally—joining our mailing list is the best way to stay updated on upcoming opportunities.
